How it works

Three steps to never wasting
half a flat of strawberries again

📝

Post it

Heading to Costco? Post what you're buying and when. Snap a photo or scan the barcode — done in under a minute.

🤝

Match up

Neighbors who shop at your Costco see your split and tap to join. Chat in the app to settle the details.

⚖️

Meet & halve

Meet at the store entrance (or a spot nearby), split the pack, split the receipt. Everybody wins — especially your fridge.
